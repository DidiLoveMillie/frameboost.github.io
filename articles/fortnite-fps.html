Best Fortnite Settings for Low-End PCs

This guide focuses on increasing FPS and reducing lag on low-end and mid-range PCs without upgrading hardware.

Recommended Graphics Settings

  • Rendering Mode: Performance
  • View Distance: Medium
  • Shadows: Off
  • Anti-Aliasing: Off
  • Effects: Low

Windows Optimization

Enable Game Mode, disable background apps, and set Fortnite to high performance in Windows graphics settings.
